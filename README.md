## Who We Are

We are **Team Tech** — an interdisciplinary engineering design team under the **[Society of Women Engineers (SWE)](https://uf.swe.org/)** at the **University of Florida**. Our mission is to give female engineers a collaborative, hands-on environment to solve real-world industry challenges while growing technically and professionally.

For nearly a decade, we've partnered with industry leaders to design, build, and optimize innovative solutions across hardware, software, and systems engineering.

---

## Core Capabilities

Our team of **15–20 undergraduate female engineers** spans multiple disciplines — ready to tackle full-stack engineering problems end to end.

<div align="center">

| 💻 Software Engineering | 🔧 Hardware & Prototyping | 📊 Analysis & Modeling |
|:---|:---|:---|

| Data visualization | Embedded systems | Structural analysis |
| Web & mobile apps | Robotics, Arduino, Raspberry Pi | Performance optimization |
